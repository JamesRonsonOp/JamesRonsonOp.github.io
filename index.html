Skills and Services

Skills and Services

Data Analytics, Processing, Engineering and Machine Learning Summary

Data Engineering, ETL and Data Pipelines

Using python, SQL, Pandas, Spark, Databricks, Google Big Query, AWS and other tools I can carefully extract data from your source(s) such as databases, IMS systems or XML files. I will then clean, prepare and validate the data for loading into your data warehouse or end target of choice.

Data Analytics and Business Intelligence

By combining the power of python with Data visualization tools such as Tableau I am able to develop and deploy dashboards and presentations that will allow your business the ability to identify trends and patterns that were not always so apparent. This will allow you to make crucial decisions with greater ease.

Data Modeling

The capability to perform a wide variety of models processing both structured and unstructured data. I have experience working with varying types of models including basic linear regression and multi-class neural nets.

Data Visualization

Matplotlib, Seaborn, Plotly, Tableau, SpaCy and Kepler.gl are among the tools used to create intuitive and interactive visualizations and dashboards.

Processes

Agile and Scrum allow me to tackle complex projects quickly and efficiently.

Libraries and Packages

Some of my favorite Python libraries and data science packages include Pandas, Sklearn, Tensorflow, SpaCy, and Geopandas.
